## Validation Questions

### Problem Validation
1. Do real estate teams actually struggle with transaction coordination?
2. Is this their #1 pain point or #10?
3. What do they currently use and why do they keep using it?
4. Would they switch tools or are they locked in?

### Solution Validation
5. Is AI contract extraction valuable or scary?
6. Do they trust AI with sensitive documents?
7. Is automation desired or do they prefer manual control?
8. What features matter vs what we built?

### Market Validation
9. Is the market big enough? (How many 3-20 agent teams exist?)
10. Can we reach them cost-effectively?
11. Will they pay $99-199/month?
12. What's the sales cycle length?

### Competitive Validation
13. Why haven't existing solutions solved this?
14. What's our actual differentiation?
15. Can incumbents copy us easily?
16. What's our moat?

## Validation Metrics

**Pre-Deployment:**
- [ ] 10 customer conversations
- [ ] 5 say "I have this problem"
- [ ] 3 say "I'd pay for this"
- [ ] 1 say "When can I start?"

**Post-Deployment (Week 1):**
- [ ] 20 signups
- [ ] 10 complete onboarding
- [ ] 5 upload a real contract
- [ ] 2 become paying customers

**Product-Market Fit (Month 1):**
- [ ] 50 paying customers
- [ ] <10% monthly churn
- [ ] 40%+ say "very disappointed" if product disappeared
- [ ] Organic referrals happening

---

## Pivot Triggers

**Pivot immediately if:**
- 10 conversations → 0 say they have this problem
- 20 signups → 0 complete onboarding
- Can't find anyone to pay even $1

**Iterate if:**
- Problem exists but solution is wrong
- Right features but wrong pricing
- Right market but wrong messaging

**Persevere if:**
- Strong problem validation
- Users asking for the product
- Early signs of PMF
