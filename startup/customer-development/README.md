## Key Findings

### The Big 3 Problems to Solve:
1. **Too Complex** - Agents don't use 80% of features because CRMs are overwhelming
2. **No Clarity** - Agents have 500+ leads but don't know who to call first
3. **Poor Mobile** - CRMs are desktop-first, agents work in the field

### Ideal Customer Profile:
- **Volume:** 20-100 deals/year
- **Team Size:** Solo to 5-person team
- **Market:** High-growth (TX, FL, AZ, CO, NC)
- **Pain:** Follow-up, lead conversion, time management
- **Tech:** Uses CRM but frustrated with it

### Best Outreach Channels:
1. LinkedIn (connection → message → call)
2. Facebook groups (engage → post → DM)
3. Twitter/X (reply to complaints → DM)
4. Reddit (observe → comment → DM)

### Top Influencer Targets:
1. Ricky Carruth (accessible, engaged audience)
2. Katie Lance (social media + systems)
3. Krista Mashore (marketing + follow-up)

## Interview Questions (Use on Calls)

### Discovery Questions:
1. "Walk me through your current follow-up process"
2. "What CRM are you using? What do you love/hate about it?"
3. "Where do leads fall through the cracks?"
4. "If you could wave a magic wand, what would your ideal system do?"
5. "What's your biggest frustration with lead management?"
6. "How do you decide who to call today?"
7. "What tools have you tried and abandoned? Why?"
8. "What would make you switch to a new CRM?"
9. "How much would you pay for a system that solved [their pain]?"
10. "Can I follow up with you when we launch to get your feedback?"
