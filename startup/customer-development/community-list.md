## Community Engagement Best Practices

### DO:
- Introduce yourself as a founder building something for agents
- Ask questions: "What's your biggest pain point with follow-up?"
- Share what you're learning (builds credibility)
- Offer to beta test with interested agents
- Join 5-10 groups max (quality over quantity)

### DON'T:
- Post promotional content early
- Drop links without context
- Automate posts (gets you banned)
- Argue with gatekeepers
- Ignore group rules
