**Purpose:** Cold outreach to real estate agents for beta user recruitment

## LinkedIn Connection Request Messages (300 char limit)

### Option 1: Personalized Observation
```
Hi [Name], I noticed your post about [specific topic - e.g., "struggling with lead follow-up"]. Building a tool to solve that exact problem for agents. Would love to connect and learn from your experience.
```

### Option 2: Brokerage/Location Tie
```
Hey [Name], saw you're crushing it with [Brokerage] in [City]. I'm building something that might help with your follow-up workflow. Mind connecting?
```

### Option 3: Direct Value-First
```
Hi [Name], I'm interviewing top agents about their biggest CRM frustrations. Would love 10 mins of your insights. Happy to share what I'm learning. Connect?
```

### Option 4: Curiosity/Flattery
```
[Name], I've been following your content on [topic]. Love your approach to [specific thing]. Building a tool inspired by agents like you - connect?
```

## LinkedIn Message After Connection (First Touch)

### Template 1: Interview Request (Best Response Rate)
**Subject:** Quick question about your CRM setup

```
Hey [Name],

Thanks for connecting!

I'm building a tool to help agents with follow-up and lead management, but I want to make sure I'm solving real problems—not imagined ones.

Would you be open to a 15-min call to share your biggest frustrations with your current setup?

In exchange, I'm happy to:
- Share insights from 30+ agent interviews I've done
- Give you early access to what I'm building (no strings)
- Venmo you $25 for your time

No pitch, just research. Would that be helpful?

[Your name]
```

```
I'm working on something to help, but in the meantime—what's working for you? Any systems or hacks that have helped?

(Drop a comment, I'd love to learn)
```

## A/B Test Variables

Test these elements to improve response rates:

### Subject Lines:
- "Question about your CRM" vs. "Quick question" vs. "15 min call?"
- Personalized vs. generic
- Question vs. statement

### CTAs:
- "Would you be open to a call?" vs. "Can I send you a link?"
- "15-minute call" vs. "Quick chat" vs. "Short conversation"
- "$25 for your time" vs. "Free early access" vs. "Share insights"

### Message Length:
- Short (50-100 words) vs. medium (100-200 words)
- Bullet points vs. paragraphs

### Tone:
- Casual/peer-to-peer vs. professional/formal
- Curiosity-driven vs. problem-agitation
