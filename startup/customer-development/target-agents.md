## Agent Selection Criteria

### Ideal Agent Profile (ICP)
- **Transaction Volume:** 20-100 deals/year (busy but accessible)
- **Team Size:** Solo to 5-person team
- **Market:** High-growth markets (TX, FL, AZ, CO, NC)
- **Tech Adoption:** Uses CRM but complains about it
- **Pain Point:** Follow-up, lead conversion, time management
- **Openness:** Active on social media, responds to messages

### Avoid:
- Brand new agents (no budget, no pain yet)
- Top 1% producers (too big, different needs)
- Agents without CRMs (not our target)
- Corporate relocation teams (long sales cycles)

## Agent Persona Notes

### Persona 1: "Overwhelmed Solo Agent"
- 30-50 deals/year
- No assistant, doing it all
- Has CRM but doesn't use it well
- Pain: "I'm dropping leads, missing follow-ups"
- Outreach angle: "I help agents like you never miss a follow-up"

### Persona 2: "Growing Team Leader"
- 100+ deals/year with 2-3 agents
- Struggling to train team on systems
- Pain: "My agents aren't following up properly"
- Outreach angle: "I help team leaders standardize follow-up"

### Persona 3: "Tech-Forward Broker Associate"
- 50-75 deals/year
- Early adopter, frustrated with tools
- Pain: "Current tools are clunky, overpriced, underwhelming"
- Outreach angle: "I'm building the tool you wish existed"
