## A Specific Example: The Johnson Family Transaction

**Transaction:** 123 Oak Street, Austin, TX (listed at $725,000)
**Timeline:** October 15 - November 28, 2025 (45-day close)
**Challenge:** Tight timeline with 4 contingencies (inspection, financing, appraisal, survey) plus a picky buyer who needed constant reassurance

The Johnson family was relocating from Seattle for a new job and needed to close quickly. They were anxious first-time Texas homebuyers with lots of questions and concerns. The transaction had 4 contingencies and a 45-day close timeline—tight by Austin standards.

Sarah uploaded the contract to Gravmap on October 15th, and within 15 seconds the AI extracted all the dates and created a timeline. She immediately shared the timeline with the Johnsons via the client update feature, which calmed their nerves about the process.

Throughout the 45 days, Gravmap sent automated weekly updates to the Johnsons explaining what was happening and what to expect next. When the inspection contingency deadline was 3 days away, Gravmap sent SMS reminders to Sarah and email reminders to the Johnsons, ensuring everyone was aligned.

The deal closed on time on November 28th, and the Johnsons were thrilled.

"The Johnsons told me it was the smoothest, most stress-free real estate experience they'd ever had," Sarah recalls. "They were so impressed by how organized we were and how well we communicated. Gravmap made me look like a rockstar. I probably spent 30 minutes total on admin for that transaction—normally it would have been 3+ hours."
