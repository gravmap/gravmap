## Content Repurposing Strategy

### One Blog Post → Multiple Pieces

**Blog Post → Repurposed As:**
1. Twitter thread (8-12 tweets summarizing key points)
2. LinkedIn carousel (5-7 slides)
3. LinkedIn long-form post
4. Facebook post (condensed version)
5. Email newsletter section
6. YouTube video script
7. Podcast talking points
8. Infographic for Pinterest
9. Guest post on industry blog (spun version)
10. Quote graphics for Instagram

### Repurposing Workflow

1. **Write blog post** (Monday)
2. **Create Twitter thread** same day
3. **Design LinkedIn carousel** (Tuesday)
4. **Record video version** (Wednesday)
5. **Include in email** (Thursday)
6. **Create quote graphics** (Friday)
7. **Syndicate to Medium** (after 1 week)
