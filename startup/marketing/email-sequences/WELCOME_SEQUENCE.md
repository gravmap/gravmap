## Email Sequence Best Practices

### Timing Guidelines

**Welcome Sequence:**
- Email 1: Immediate
- Email 2: +24 hours
- Email 3: +48 hours
- Email 4: +72 hours
- Email 5: +7 days

**Trial Conversion:**
- Email 1: Day 7 (halfway)
- Email 2: Day 11 (3 days before end)
- Email 3: Day 15 (day after trial ends)

**Re-engagement:**
- Email 1: 30 days inactive
- Email 2: 7 days later
- Email 3: 14 days later

### Personalization Tokens

Use these tokens in emails:
- `[First Name]` - User's first name
- `[Last Name]` - User's last name
- `[Email]` - User's email
- `[Company]` - Team/company name
- `[Trial End Date]` - Date trial ends
- `[Days Remaining]` - Days left in trial
- `[Plan Name]` - Current plan name
- `[Upgrade Link]` - Link to upgrade page
- `[Login Link]` - Link to login page

### A/B Test Ideas

**Subject Lines:**
- Test urgency vs. curiosity
- Test emoji vs. no emoji
- Test question vs. statement
- Test personalization (first name) vs. generic

**Email Content:**
- Test short vs. long emails
- Test one CTA vs. multiple CTAs
- Test video link vs. no video
- Test social proof vs. no social proof
- Test discount vs. no discount

**Timing:**
- Test morning vs. afternoon
- Test weekday vs. weekend
- Test shorter vs. longer intervals

### Key Metrics to Track

**Per Email:**
- Open rate (target: 40-60%)
- Click rate (target: 10-20%)
- Reply rate (for feedback emails)
- Unsubscribe rate (target: <0.5%)

**Per Sequence:**
- Overall conversion rate (trial → paid)
- Time to conversion
- Revenue per email sent

**Segmented by:**
- Customer segment (solo vs. team)
- Signup source
- Engagement level
- Plan type
