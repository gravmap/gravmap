## 1. Product Hunt Launch Strategy

### Why Product Hunt?

- High-visibility platform for tech products
- Early adopter audience
- SEO benefits (backlinks, traffic)
- Credibility signal for investors/customers
- Free marketing if successful

### Goals

| Metric | Good | Great | Outstanding |
|--------|------|-------|-------------|
| Upvotes | 200+ | 500+ | 1,000+ |
| Comments | 50+ | 100+ | 200+ |

## 7. Contingency Plans

### If Product Hunt Flops (< 100 upvotes)

**Immediate Actions:**
1. Don't panic - it's just one channel
2. Focus on other channels (press, communities)
3. Analyze what went wrong (timing, messaging, product)
4. Consider re-launching in 30+ days with improvements
5. Document learnings for next time

### If Website Crashes

**Immediate Actions:**
1. Have backup landing page ready (Carrd, etc.)
2. Switch DNS if needed
3. Communicate on social: "We're experiencing high traffic!"
4. Redirect traffic to email signup if site down
5. Fix ASAP, communicate when back

### If Negative Feedback

**Immediate Actions:**
1. Respond publicly and professionally
2. Take conversation to DM if detailed
3. Acknowledge valid concerns
4. Explain what you're fixing
5. Follow up when fixed
6. Don't delete negative comments (looks bad)

### If Press Ignores

**Alternative Approaches:**
1. Write your own article (Medium, LinkedIn)
2. Double down on community marketing
3. Reach out to smaller publications
4. Guest post on industry blogs
5. Focus on customer stories/testimonials
