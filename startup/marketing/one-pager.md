# Gravmap - One-Pager

## The AI Transaction Coordinator That Never Misses a Deadline

---

### What We Do

**Gravmap** is an AI-native workflow automation platform for real estate teams (3-20 agents) that autonomously handles transaction coordination, deadline management, and client communication from contract to close.

**How It Works:**
1. Upload contract PDF
2. AI extracts critical dates, contacts, contingencies
3. Auto-generates timeline with deadline tracking
4. Sends smart reminders before every deadline
5. Drafts client status updates (one-click send)

**The Result:** Agents spend **10+ fewer hours per transaction** on admin work and **never miss a deadline**.
