# Paid Advertising Strategy

**Created:** March 17, 2026  
**Platforms:** Google Ads, Facebook/Instagram, LinkedIn

## Paid Ads Overview

### Strategy Phases

| Phase | Timeline | Budget | Focus |
|-------|----------|--------|-------|
| Phase 1: Testing | Month 1-2 | $200-500/mo | Message testing, audience discovery |
| Phase 2: Optimization | Month 3-4 | $500-1,500/mo | Scale winning campaigns |
| Phase 3: Scale | Month 5-12 | $2,000-5,000/mo | Full-funnel acquisition |

### Target Metrics

| Metric | Phase 1 | Phase 2 | Phase 3 |
|--------|---------|---------|---------|
| Cost Per Lead (CPL) | <$30 | <$25 | <$20 |
| Cost Per Trial Signup | <$50 | <$40 | <$30 |
| Cost Per Paid Customer (CAC) | <$150 | <$120 | <$100 |
| Trial-to-Paid Rate | 20% | 25% | 30%+ |
| ROAS | 1.5x | 2x | 3x+ |

## Retargeting Strategy

### Retargeting Audiences

| Audience | Source | Window | Priority |
|----------|--------|--------|----------|
| Homepage Visitors | Pixel | 30 days | Medium |
| Blog Readers | Pixel | 14 days | Low |
| Pricing Page | Pixel | 30 days | High |
| Trial Signups (Not Converted) | Pixel + CRM | 90 days | Very High |
| Trial Activated | Pixel | 14 days | High |

### Retargeting Sequences

**Sequence 1: Homepage Visitors (7-day sequence)**
- Day 1: General awareness ad
- Day 3: Social proof ad (testimonial)
- Day 5: Feature highlight (deadline tracking)
- Day 7: Offer/founders pricing

**Sequence 2: Pricing Page Visitors (5-day sequence)**
- Day 1: ROI/cost comparison
- Day 2: Testimonial
- Day 4: FOMO (limited spots)
- Day 5: Final offer

**Sequence 3: Trial Signups - Not Activated**
- Day 1: "Need help getting started?"
- Day 3: "Here's how to upload your first contract"
- Day 5: Case study / success story
- Day 7: "Last chance" / upgrade reminder

**Sequence 4: Trial Ending Soon (3-day sequence)**
- Day 3: "Your trial ends in 3 days"
- Day 2: "Here's what you've accomplished"
- Day 1: "Last chance to save 50%"

## Creative Testing Framework

### What to Test

**Headlines:**
1. Pain point vs. benefit
2. Question vs. statement
3. Short vs. long
4. Feature vs. outcome

**Images:**
1. Product screenshot vs. lifestyle
2. Person vs. no person
3. Testimonial vs. benefit graphic
4. Video vs. static

**CTAs:**
1. "Start Free Trial" vs. "Learn More"
2. "Try Free" vs. "Get Started"
3. "Claim Offer" vs. "See Pricing"

**Copy Length:**
1. Short (2 sentences) vs. Long (4+ sentences)
2. Feature-focused vs. benefit-focused

### Testing Rules

- Test 1 variable at a time
- Run tests for minimum 7 days
- Require minimum 100 clicks per variation
- Statistical significance threshold: 90%+
- Document all tests and results

## Attribution & Tracking

### UTM Parameters

**Standard Format:**
```
?utm_source=[platform]&utm_medium=[cpc/social/display]&utm_campaign=[campaign_name]&utm_content=[ad_name]&utm_term=[keyword]
```

**Example:**
```
?utm_source=google&utm_medium=cpc&utm_campaign=transaction_coordinator&utm_content=ad1_h1&utm_term=transaction+coordinator+software
```

### Conversion Tracking

**Events to Track:**
1. Page views (home, pricing, features)
2. Trial signup start
3. Trial signup complete
4. Contract upload
5. First timeline generated
6. Payment added
7. Subscription started

**Platforms:**
- Google Ads: Conversion tracking + GA4
- Facebook: Meta Pixel + Conversions API
- LinkedIn: Insight Tag

### Attribution Model

- **Initial:** Last click (simple, baseline)
- **Month 3+:** Data-driven (if enough data)
- **Reporting:** Multi-touch (for analysis)
