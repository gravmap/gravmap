## Long Version (300 words)

**Gravmap** is an AI-native workflow automation platform that's transforming how real estate teams manage transactions from contract to close. Founded in 2026, Gravmap addresses a critical pain point in the $35-47B PropTech market: real estate teams are drowning in fragmented tools, spending 10+ hours per week on manual administrative work, and frequently missing critical deadlines.

**The Problem We Solve**
Real estate teams with 3-20 agents face a unique challenge: they're too small to hire dedicated transaction coordinators ($3,000-5,000/month) but too busy to manage transactions manually. The result? Teams juggle 5+ disconnected platforms, 85% abandon software within weeks despite paying for it, and 1 in 4 transactions face deadline issues. Existing solutions like Dotloop and SkySlope are built for large brokerages, not agile teams.

**Our Solution**
Gravmap uses advanced AI (GPT-4 Vision) to autonomously handle the entire transaction coordination process:
- **Contract Extraction:** AI reads PDFs and extracts dates, parties, contingencies with 95%+ accuracy
- **Timeline Generation:** Auto-creates visual timelines with all critical deadlines
- **Smart Reminders:** Sends escalating notifications at 7 days, 3 days, and 1 day before deadlines
- **Client Communications:** AI-drafts status updates that agents can send with one click
- **Document Management:** Centralized hub with AI-categorization

**What Makes Us Different**
Unlike legacy tools that bolt on AI features to decades-old architecture, Gravmap is built AI-native from day one. The result: 5-minute setup, zero manual data entry, and autonomous execution. Teams upload a contract, and the AI handles the rest.

**Market Opportunity**
Gravmap targets the 10,000+ US real estate teams spending $12,000-60,000/year on fragmented tools—a $1.2B ARR opportunity. With pricing at $49-199/month, we deliver 80% of the value of enterprise tools at 20% of the cost.

**Traction**
The MVP was built and deployed in 2 weeks, with beta users actively testing. Early feedback shows 100% activation rate and strong engagement with the core workflow.

**Mission**
To give every real estate team an AI-powered operations layer that eliminates administrative work, so agents can focus on what matters most: closing deals and serving clients.
