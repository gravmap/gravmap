## Press Release Best Practices

### Writing Guidelines

**Structure:**
1. **Headline:** Clear, concise, benefit-focused (under 100 characters)
2. **Subheadline:** Supporting detail (under 150 characters)
3. **Dateline:** City, State – Date
4. **Lead paragraph:** Who, what, when, where, why (most important info)
5. **Body paragraphs:** Supporting details, quotes, features
6. **Boilerplate:** Standard company description
7. **Media contact:** Name, email, phone

**Content Tips:**
- Lead with the news, not the company
- Use clear, simple language (avoid jargon)
- Include 1-2 customer quotes (add credibility)
- Include 1 founder/executive quote
- Keep sentences short (under 25 words)
- Keep paragraphs short (3-4 sentences)
- Use bullet points for lists
- End with a clear call-to-action (website, trial sign-up)

**Formatting:**
- Use "FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE" at the top
- Use "###" or "# # #" to mark the end
- Keep total length to 400-500 words
- Include company boilerplate
- Always include media contact

### Distribution Checklist

**Before Distribution:**
- ✅ All [BRACKETED TEXT] replaced with actual information
- ✅ Reviewed by founder/CEO
- ✅ Approved customer quotes
- ✅ Checked for accuracy (names, titles, numbers)
- ✅ Included media contact information
- ✅ Added to company website (press page)
- ✅ Prepared supporting materials (screenshots, logos)

**Distribution Channels:**
- **Wire Services:** PR Newswire, Business Wire, GlobeNewswire
- **Free Options:** PRLog, OpenPR, 24-7 Press Release
- **Targeted:** Industry publications (Inman, HousingWire, PropTech media)
- **Direct:** Email to targeted journalists/bloggers
- **Social:** LinkedIn, Twitter with relevant hashtags
- **Owned:** Company blog, website press page, email to customers

**Timing:**
- Distribute Tuesday-Thursday (avoid Mondays/Fridays)
- Early morning (8-10 AM Eastern) preferred
- Avoid major holidays and news-heavy days
- Coordinate with product launch timing

### Follow-Up

**After Distribution:**
- Monitor coverage and pickup
- Share on social media channels
- Thank journalists who cover the story
- Update press page on website with links to coverage
- Track results (coverage, traffic, sign-ups)

**Pitching to Journalists:**
- Personalize emails (don't mass blast)
- Lead with why this matters to their readers
- Keep initial pitch short (under 150 words)
- Include link to full press release
- Offer interviews with founder/executives
- Provide customer references if requested
- Follow up once after 2-3 days (don't spam)
