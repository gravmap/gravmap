### Screenshot Technical Requirements

**File Formats:**
- **Web use:** PNG (lossless, transparent background if needed) or JPG (smaller file size)
- **Print use:** PNG or TIFF at 300 DPI
- **Social media:** PNG, optimized for platform

**Resolution Guidelines:**
- **Desktop screenshots:** 1920x1080px minimum (16:9 aspect ratio)
- **Mobile screenshots:** 750x1334px (iPhone) or 1080x1920px (Android)
- **Print:** 300 DPI minimum

**Best Practices:**
- Use realistic sample data (not lorem ipsum)
- Avoid personal/sensitive information
- Show diverse sample transactions
- Ensure consistent branding
- Use high-quality, professional design

**File Naming Convention:**
- `gravmap-screenshot-[description]-[size].png`
- Example: `gravmap-screenshot-dashboard-1920x1080.png`
