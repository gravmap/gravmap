### FAQ

**Q: What file formats are supported?**
A: We support PDF files (most common for real estate contracts). Maximum file size: 25MB.

**Q: How long does extraction take?**
A: Typically 10-15 seconds for a standard purchase agreement. Complex contracts may take up to 30 seconds.

**Q: What if the AI makes a mistake?**
A: You always review the extraction before confirming. Any field can be edited. Your corrections help the AI learn.

**Q: Is my contract data secure?**
A: Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never share your contract information with third parties. You own your data.

**Q: Can I extract data from handwritten contracts?**
A: GPT-4 Vision can handle some handwritten text if it's legible, but typed contracts have higher accuracy (95%+ vs. 70-80% for handwritten).

**Q: Do you support contracts in other languages?**
A: Currently we only support English-language contracts. Multi-language support is on our roadmap.

**Q: How many contracts can I upload?**
A: Starter plan: 15 active transactions. Professional and Team plans: unlimited.

**Q: Can I re-upload a contract if I need to update it?**
A: Yes! You can upload a new version of a contract at any time, and we'll re-extract the data.

### Reminder Types

**Headline:**
The Right Reminder at the Right Time

**Grid of Reminder Types:**

**7 Days Before - "Heads Up"**
- **Tone:** Friendly, informational
- **Purpose:** Awareness and planning
- **Example:** "Hi [Name], your inspection contingency deadline is in 7 days on [Date]. Plan accordingly!"

**3 Days Before - "Take Action"**
- **Tone:** Urgent, action-oriented
- **Purpose:** Prompt action
- **Example:** "Hi [Name], your inspection contingency deadline is in 3 days on [Date]. Please complete inspection and respond by this date."

**1 Day Before - "URGENT"**
- **Tone:** Critical, immediate
- **Purpose:** Final warning
- **Example:** "URGENT: Hi [Name], your inspection contingency deadline is TOMORROW on [Date]. Action required TODAY to avoid default."

**Same Day - "Today's the Day"**
- **Tone:** Critical
- **Purpose:** Final call
- **Example:** "TODAY: Hi [Name], your inspection contingency deadline is today. Complete this action immediately."
