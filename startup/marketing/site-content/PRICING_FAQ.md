# Gravmap Pricing Page FAQ

## Pricing Questions

### General Pricing

**Q: How much does Gravmap cost?**
A: Gravmap offers three plans:
- **Starter:** $49/month (15 active transactions, 1 user)
- **Professional:** $99/month (unlimited transactions, 3 users, SMS)
- **Team:** $199/month (unlimited transactions, 10 users, phone support, custom workflows)

All plans include a 14-day free trial with no credit card required.

**Q: Is there a setup fee?**
A: No. Zero setup fees, zero hidden costs. You only pay the monthly subscription fee.

**Q: Do you offer annual billing discounts?**
A: Yes! Save 20% with annual billing (essentially 2 months free):
- Starter: $470/year (save $118)
- Professional: $950/year (save $238)
- Team: $1,900/year (save $476)

**Q: Can I switch between monthly and annual billing?**
A: Yes. You can switch to annual billing at any time and we'll prorate the difference. If you switch from annual to monthly, the change takes effect at your next billing date.

**Q: Are there any hidden fees?**
A: Absolutely not. The price you see is the price you pay. No per-transaction fees, no per-user fees beyond what's included, no surprise charges.

### Choosing a Plan

**Q: How do I know which plan is right for me?**
A: Here's a quick guide:

**Choose Starter ($49/month) if:**
- You're a solo agent
- You close fewer than 15 transactions per year
- You want basic deadline tracking and reminders
- Email support is sufficient

**Choose Professional ($99/month) if:** ⭐ Most Popular
- You're a producing agent closing 15-50+ transactions/year
- You want unlimited transactions
- You need SMS notifications
- You want Google Calendar integration
- You have a small team (up to 3 users)

**Choose Team ($199/month) if:**
- You have a team of 3-20 agents
- You close 50+ transactions/year
- You want phone support
- You need custom workflows
- You want team analytics and reporting

**Still not sure?** Start with Professional during your trial—you can always downgrade to Starter or upgrade to Team later.

**Q: What counts as an "active transaction"?**
A: An active transaction is any transaction that is currently in progress (not yet closed). Once a transaction is marked as "closed," it no longer counts toward your limit. Closed transactions are archived but still accessible.

**Q: What if I exceed my transaction limit on the Starter plan?**
A: You have two options:
1. Upgrade to Professional (unlimited transactions)
2. Close/archive older transactions to free up space

You'll receive a notification when you're approaching your limit so there are no surprises.

**Q: Can I have multiple users on one account?**
A: Yes. Here's how users work:
- **Starter:** 1 user (you)
- **Professional:** Up to 3 users
- **Team:** Up to 10 users

Each user gets their own login and can access shared transactions. Additional users beyond the included amount are not available—if you need more users, upgrade to the next plan.

**Q: What's the difference between email support, priority support, and phone support?**
A:
- **Email support (Starter):** Response within 24 hours on business days
- **Priority email support (Professional):** Response within 4 hours on business days
- **Phone + priority email support (Team):** Response within 2 hours + phone support during business hours

### Billing and Payments

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover) via Stripe. We also accept ACH bank transfers for annual plans (contact us for details).

**Q: When will I be charged?**
A: For monthly plans, you're charged on the same date each month (your billing anniversary). For annual plans, you're charged once per year on your signup date.

**Q: Can I get an invoice for my subscription?**
A: Yes. Invoices are automatically generated and available in your account settings. You can add your company name, address, and tax ID for proper invoicing.

**Q: Do you offer refunds?**
A: Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied for any reason within your first 30 days of paid subscription, contact us and we'll refund your payment in full. No questions asked.

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**
A: Yes. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees. Cancel anytime from your account settings, and your subscription will end at your current billing period. You won't be charged again.

**Q: What happens to my data if I cancel?**
A: If you cancel, your data is retained for 90 days. During this time, you can reactivate your account and regain access. After 90 days, your data is permanently deleted.

**Q: Do you prorate refunds if I cancel mid-month?**
A: No, but you retain access until the end of your current billing period. For example, if you cancel on the 15th of the month but paid through the 30th, you can use Gravmap until the 30th.

**Q: Can I pause my subscription instead of canceling?**
A: Not currently, but you can cancel and reactivate within 90 days without losing your data.

### Upgrading and Downgrading

**Q: Can I change plans later?**
A: Yes. Upgrade or downgrade anytime from your account settings. Changes take effect immediately.

**Q: How does billing work when I upgrade?**
A: When you upgrade, you're immediately charged the prorated difference for the remainder of your billing period. For example, if you upgrade from Starter ($49) to Professional ($99) halfway through the month, you'll be charged $25 (half the difference).

**Q: How does billing work when I downgrade?**
A: When you downgrade, you remain on your current plan until the end of your billing period. Then, the new lower rate applies. No refunds or credits for the difference.

**Q: Will I lose my data if I downgrade?**
A: No, you won't lose data. However, if you downgrade to Starter and have more than 15 active transactions, you'll need to close/archive transactions to meet the limit.

**Q: Can I upgrade or downgrade from annual to monthly?**
A: You can switch between plans (e.g., Starter to Professional) anytime. Switching from annual to monthly billing happens at your next renewal date. Switching from monthly to annual can happen immediately (with prorated credit for time remaining).

### Security and Data

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. We take security seriously:
- **Encryption:** 256-bit SSL encryption in transit, AES-256 encryption at rest
- **Infrastructure:** SOC 2 Type II compliant (via Supabase)
- **Access control:** Row-level security, you own your data
- **Backups:** Daily automated backups with 30-day retention
- **Compliance:** GDPR compliant data handling

**Q: Who owns my transaction data?**
A: You own your data. Gravmap is just the processor. We never share or sell your data to third parties.

**Q: Where is my data stored?**
A: Data is stored in secure, US-based data centers via Supabase (SOC 2 Type II certified).

**Q: Do you share my contract data with third parties?**
A: No. Your contract data is used only to provide Gravmap services (extraction, timelines, etc.). We do not share, sell, or use your data for any other purpose.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Yes. Export all your transaction data, documents, and timelines at any time from your account settings. Available in CSV and JSON formats.

**Q: What happens to my data if Gravmap goes out of business?**
A: In the unlikely event Gravmap ceases operations, you'll receive advance notice and an opportunity to export all your data. We're committed to ensuring you always have access to your information.
