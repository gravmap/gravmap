## Twitter/X Strategy

### Platform-Specific Goals
- Build a following of engaged real estate professionals
- Establish thought leadership in real estate tech
- Drive traffic to website and trial signups
- Engage in relevant conversations

### Content Mix
- **40% Educational:** Tips, how-tos, industry insights
- **25% Engagement:** Questions, polls, replies
- **20% Product:** Features, updates, demos
- **15% Brand:** Company updates, team, values

### Post Types

**1. Threads (2-3x/week)**
- Educational deep-dives
- How-to guides
- Industry analysis
- Product walkthroughs

**2. Single Tweets (3-5x/day)**
- Quick tips
- Statistics
- Questions
- Replies to others
- Product highlights

**3. Visual Tweets (1-2x/day)**
- Screenshots
- Infographics
- Charts
- GIFs

### Best Practices
- Post at optimal times: 8-10am, 12-1pm, 5-6pm ET
- Use 1-3 hashtags max per tweet
- Engage with replies within 1 hour
- Quote tweet industry news with commentary
- Use Twitter Spaces for live Q&A (1x/month)

### Key Metrics
- Impressions: Target 10,000+/week
- Engagement rate: Target 3%+
- Followers: Target 1,000+ by Month 3
- Link clicks: Target 100+/week

### Post Templates

**Educational Tip:**
```
[Hook - surprising fact or bold statement]

[Context - why this matters]

[Tip - actionable advice]

[Result - what happens if you do this]

[Question to encourage engagement]

#RealEstate #PropTech
```

**Thread:**
```
🧵 [COMPELLING HOOK]

[Problem statement]

Here's what you need to know:

1/ [First point]

2/ [Second point]

[Continue for 8-12 tweets]

[Final tweet - Summary + CTA]

If this was helpful:
• Retweet the first tweet
• Follow for more
• Check out [link]
```

**Product Highlight:**
```
✨ New Feature Alert

[Feature name]

[What it does - 1 line]

[Why it matters - benefit]

[How to use - quick instruction]

Try it free: [link]

#PropTech
```

## LinkedIn Strategy

### Platform-Specific Goals
- Build professional credibility
- Reach decision-makers (team leaders, brokers)
- Generate leads through thought leadership
- Establish founder/company presence

### Content Mix
- **35% Thought Leadership:** Industry insights, opinions
- **25% Educational:** How-tos, tips, guides
- **20% Company Updates:** Product news, milestones
- **15% Personal:** Founder journey, behind scenes
- **5% Promotional:** Direct product promotion

### Post Types

**1. Long-form Posts (1x/day)**
- 1,000-1,500 characters
- Story-driven
- Professional but personal

**2. Document/Carousel Posts (2-3x/week)**
- Slide decks
- Guides
- Infographics

**3. Video Posts (2-3x/week)**
- Product demos
- Founder updates
- Tips

**4. Polls (1-2x/week)**
- Industry questions
- Feature feedback
- Opinion gathering

**5. Article Publishing (1x/week)**
- Long-form content
- Repurposed blog posts
- Original insights

### Best Practices
- Post at 8-10am or 12-1pm on weekdays
- Use relevant hashtags (3-5 per post)
- Engage with comments within 2 hours
- Tag relevant people sparingly
- Use "View post" to maximize reach

### Key Metrics
- Impressions: Target 5,000+/post
- Engagement rate: Target 5%+
- Followers: Target 500+ by Month 3
- Profile views: Target 200+/week

### Post Templates

## Facebook Strategy

### Platform-Specific Goals
- Build community presence
- Engage in real estate agent groups
- Drive organic referrals
- Support and customer service

### Content Mix (Page)
- **40% Educational:** Tips, guides, how-tos
- **25% Engagement:** Questions, polls, discussions
- **20% Visual:** Graphics, photos, videos
- **15% Promotional:** Product updates, offers

### Content Mix (Groups)
- **70% Value:** Answer questions, share tips, help others
- **20% Relationship:** Personal updates, celebrations
- **10% Subtle Promotion:** Mention product when relevant

### Post Types

**1. Image Posts (1x/day)**
- Tips graphics
- Infographics
- Product screenshots
- Motivational quotes

**2. Video Posts (2-3x/week)**
- Product demos
- Tips
- Live Q&As

**3. Link Posts (3-4x/week)**
- Blog posts
- Industry articles
- Product updates

**4. Text + Question (2-3x/week)**
- Discussion starters
- Polls
- Feedback requests

### Group Engagement Strategy

**Target Groups (Join 10-15 active groups):**
- Real Estate Agents United
- Real Estate Marketing
- Real Estate Tech & Tools
- Realtors Supporting Realtors
- Team Building for Real Estate
- Location-specific groups (TX, FL, AZ, CO, NC)

**Group Engagement Rules:**
1. Provide value before promoting (10:1 ratio)
2. Answer questions thoroughly
3. Share helpful resources (not just our content)
4. Build relationships, don't spam
5. Follow each group's self-promotion rules
6. Mention product organically when solving a problem

**Weekly Group Activities:**
- Answer 20+ questions across groups
- Share 5+ valuable tips
- Post 1-2 pieces of original content
- Celebrate others' wins
- Engage with top contributors

### Best Practices
- Post at 9am, 1pm, 7pm (agent schedules)
- Use native video (better reach than YouTube links)
- Respond to all comments within 4 hours
- Use Facebook Live for product demos (1x/week)
- Create Facebook Group for customers (Month 2)

### Key Metrics
- Page reach: Target 5,000+/week
- Post engagement: Target 5%+
- Group engagement: 50+ helpful interactions/week
- Referral traffic: Target 100+/week

## Engagement Tactics

### Daily Engagement Routine (30 min)

**Morning (10 min):**
- [ ] Check notifications and respond
- [ ] Engage with 5 posts from target accounts
- [ ] Share 1 industry article with commentary

**Afternoon (10 min):**
- [ ] Reply to all new comments
- [ ] Engage in 3 Facebook group discussions
- [ ] Send 5 LinkedIn connection requests

**Evening (10 min):**
- [ ] Final comment responses
- [ ] Schedule tomorrow's posts
- [ ] Note trending topics for content ideas

### Target Accounts to Engage With

**Real Estate Coaches/Influencers:**
- Ricky Carruth (@RickyCarruth)
- Tom Ferry (@TomFerry)
- Katie Lance (@KatieLance)
- Krista Mashore (@KristaMashore)
- Jason Pantana (@JasonPantana)

**PropTech Leaders:**
- Follow competitors and engage thoughtfully
- Industry analysts and journalists
- Real estate tech podcast hosts

**Target Customers:**
- Active agents in target markets
- Team leaders discussing operations
- Agents complaining about current tools

### Engagement Guidelines

**DO:**
- Add genuine value in comments
- Ask thoughtful questions
- Share others' content with credit
- Celebrate others' wins
- Offer help without expectation
- Be consistent and patient

**DON'T:**
- Spam comments with promotional links
- Copy-paste the same comment
- Engage just to get attention
- Be negative or critical of competitors
- Over-tag people
- Buy followers or engagement
