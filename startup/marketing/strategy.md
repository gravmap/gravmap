# Marketing Strategy - AI-Native Real Estate Workflow Automation

**Version:** 1.0  
**Created:** March 17, 2026  
**Status:** Ready for Execution

## Executive Summary

**Product:** AI-native workflow automation platform for real estate teams that autonomously handles transaction coordination, deadline management, and client communication.

**Core Message:** "Stop managing tools. Start closing deals."

**Target Market:** Real estate teams with 3-20 agents in the United States, producing teams closing 50-500 transactions/year.

**Pricing:** $49/month (Starter), $99/month (Professional), $199/month (Team)

**Launch Goal:** 50 paying customers, $2.5K MRR in 90 days

## 1. Target Audience Personas

### Persona 1: "The Overwhelmed Solo Agent" 🏃‍♂️

**Demographics:**
- Age: 32-45
- Gender: Mix (slightly female-skewed in RE)
- Income: $80,000-150,000/year
- Location: High-growth markets (TX, FL, AZ, CO, NC)
- Transaction volume: 30-50 deals/year

**Professional Context:**
- Works alone, no assistant
- Has been in real estate 3-8 years
- Uses a CRM but barely (Follow Up Boss or kvCORE)
- Juggling 5+ tools: CRM, transaction software, marketing, email, calendar
- Feeling burned out from admin work

**Psychographics:**
- **Values:** Freedom, efficiency, work-life balance
- **Fears:** Missing deadlines, losing clients, falling behind
- **Motivations:** Wants to focus on selling, not paperwork
- **Frustrations:** "I'm working 60 hours but only 20 are actual selling"

**Pain Points:**
1. Drowning in paperwork and admin tasks
2. Constantly missing follow-ups with clients
3. Can't afford a transaction coordinator ($3,000-5,000/month)
4. Embarrassed when clients ask "What's the status?"
5. Feels like they're always putting out fires

**Goals:**
- Save 10+ hours/week on admin work
- Never miss a deadline or follow-up again
- Appear more professional and organized to clients
- Get evenings and weekends back

**Decision Drivers:**
- Price sensitivity: Medium ($100-200/month acceptable)
- Needs: Quick setup, mobile-friendly, "set it and forget it"
- Influencers: Peers in mastermind groups, Facebook groups
- Objections: "Will this actually save me time or add more complexity?"

**Buying Process:**
- Discovers through: Facebook group recommendation, LinkedIn, podcast
- Researches: Watches demo video, reads reviews, checks pricing
- Converts: 14-day free trial → concierge onboarding call → first transaction uploaded

**Messaging Hooks:**
- "Your AI transaction coordinator that never misses a deadline"
- "Upload your contract. We handle the rest."
- "What if you could get 10 hours of your week back?"

## 4. Channel Strategy

### Channel Mix Overview

| Channel | Type | Priority | Budget % | Expected ROI |
|---------|------|----------|----------|--------------|
| LinkedIn | Organic + Paid | HIGH | 25% | 3-5x |
| Facebook Groups | Organic | HIGH | 0% | 5-10x |
| Content/SEO | Organic | HIGH | 15% | 10x+ |
| Referrals | Organic | HIGH | 10% | 10x+ |
| Google Ads | Paid | MEDIUM | 20% | 2-3x |
| Facebook Ads | Paid | MEDIUM | 15% | 2-3x |
| Influencer/Coach Partnerships | Partnership | MEDIUM | 10% | 3-5x |
| Product Hunt | Organic | ONE-TIME | 0% | 5-10x |
| Email | Owned | HIGH | 5% | 5-10x |

### Phase 1: Months 1-3 (First 100 Customers)

**Primary Channels:**
1. **Direct Outreach (LinkedIn)** - 40% of customers
   - 50 connection requests/week
   - Personalized messages, not spam
   - Video demos via Loom
   - Concierge onboarding calls

2. **Community Marketing (Facebook)** - 30% of customers
   - Join 10 active RE agent groups
   - Provide value before promoting
   - Share success stories, templates
   - Become known as "the helpful one"

3. **Referrals** - 20% of customers
   - Launch referral program (1 month free per referral)
   - Ask satisfied customers for intros
   - Affiliate program for coaches/consultants

4. **Content/SEO** - 10% of customers
   - 2 blog posts/week
   - YouTube tutorials
   - Social content (daily)
   - SEO foundation building

### Phase 2: Months 4-6 (Scaling to 500 Customers)

**Add Channels:**
5. **Google Ads** - Retargeting + bottom-funnel keywords
6. **Facebook/Instagram Ads** - Agent targeting
7. **Influencer Partnerships** - 2-3 coach partnerships
8. **Product Hunt Launch** - Big visibility spike

### Phase 3: Months 7-12 (Growth to 1,000+ Customers)

**Scale Channels:**
- Increase paid ad spend based on ROI data
- Launch affiliate program (20-30% recurring commission)
- Expand to secondary markets
- Consider podcast sponsorship
- Conference presence
