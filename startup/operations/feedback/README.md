# Customer Feedback System

**Purpose:** Collect, analyze, and act on customer feedback to continuously improve the product and customer experience

**Goal:** Build a feedback loop that makes customers feel heard and drives product development

---

## Components

1. **Feedback Collection Mechanism** - Multiple channels for gathering input
2. **NPS Survey** - Net Promoter Score measurement
3. **Feature Request Template** - Standardized format for capturing requests
4. **Churn Interview Script** - Exit interviews to understand why customers leave

---

## Feedback Philosophy

### The "Feedback Loop" Model

```
Collect → Analyze → Act → Communicate → Repeat
```

1. **Collect:** Gather feedback from multiple channels
2. **Analyze:** Identify patterns, priorities, and actionable insights
3. **Act:** Make product/process improvements based on feedback
4. **Communicate:** Tell customers what you did (closes the loop)
5. **Repeat:** Continuous cycle

### Key Principles

1. **Make it easy:** Remove friction from giving feedback
2. **Respond to everything:** Acknowledge every piece of feedback
3. **Close the loop:** Tell customers what happened with their feedback
4. **Prioritize transparently:** Share how decisions are made
5. **Thank them:** Show appreciation for their time
