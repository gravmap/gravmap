## Content Standards

### Writing Guidelines

1. **Use plain language** - No jargon, simple words
2. **Be concise** - Get to the point quickly
3. **Use screenshots** - Visual aids for complex steps
4. **Number steps** - Scannable, actionable
5. **Include examples** - Real-world scenarios
6. **Link to related content** - Help them explore

### Article Structure

```markdown
# [Clear, Descriptive Title]

[1-sentence summary of what this article covers]

## Overview
[2-3 sentences providing context]

## [Main Content]
[Step-by-step instructions or explanation]

## Troubleshooting (if applicable)
[Common issues and solutions]

## Related Articles
- [Link 1]
- [Link 2]
- [Link 3]

---
**Was this helpful?** [Yes] [No]
```
