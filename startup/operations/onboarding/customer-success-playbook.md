## 1. Onboarding Call Scripts

### Call Structure (15-20 minutes)

#### Opening (2 minutes)
"Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] from [Product Name]. Thanks for taking the time to chat! I want to make sure you get maximum value from the platform, so I'm here to answer any questions and help you get set up. How familiar are you with the product so far?"

**If new:** "Great, let me walk you through the basics..."
**If tried already:** "Awesome, what have you explored so far? Any questions?"

---

#### Discovery (3 minutes)
"Before we dive in, tell me a bit about your workflow:

1. How many transactions are you typically managing at once?
2. What's your biggest pain point right now - deadlines, client communication, document management?
3. What tools are you currently using? (CRM, transaction software, etc.)"

**Take notes:** Log in CRM/support tool for personalization later.

---

#### Demo + Setup (10 minutes)
"Perfect. Let me show you how [Product Name] can solve [their pain point]."

**Scenario A: Deadline Management Pain**
- Show contract upload
- Walk through AI extraction
- Highlight timeline view
- Demonstrate reminder system
- Show daily digest email

**Scenario B: Client Communication Pain**
- Show contract upload
- Jump to client communication feature
- Demonstrate AI-drafted email
- Show customization options
- Emphasize time savings

**Scenario C: Document Organization Pain**
- Show document hub
- Demonstrate upload + categorization
- Show search functionality
- Explain retention and access

---

#### Action Items (3 minutes)
"Alright, here's what I'd recommend for your next steps:

1. Upload your next active contract (not a past one - you'll want the real experience)
2. Review the AI extraction
3. Send your first client update

**Can you commit to trying that this week?**"

**If yes:** "Perfect! I'll follow up in 3 days to see how it went."
**If hesitant:** "What's holding you back? Let's troubleshoot now."

---

#### Closing (2 minutes)
"Do you have any other questions for me? 

Here's how to reach me:
- Email: [your email]
- In-app chat: Click the ? icon
- Book another call: [calendar link]

I'm here to help you succeed. Looking forward to hearing how it goes!"

---

### Objection Handling During Calls

**"I don't have time to learn a new tool."**
> "Totally understand - that's exactly why we built this. The setup is 5 minutes, and then it saves you hours every week. It's like hiring a transaction coordinator for 1% of the cost. Can I show you how quick it is?"

**"I'm already using [Dotloop/SkySlope/Other]."**
> "That's great - a lot of our customers come from those tools. The difference is they require you to do all the work - entering data, setting up reminders, writing emails. We automate all of that. Think of us as an AI assistant that works alongside your existing tools. Want to see the difference?"

**"I'm not tech-savvy."**
> "No problem - if you can drag and drop a file, you can use this. Watch this 60-second demo..." [Show simplest workflow]

**"I need to think about it."**
> "Of course. What specifically do you want to think through? Is it the cost, the fit for your workflow, or something else? I want to make sure you have all the info you need."

**"It's too expensive for me."**

>

**"I can do this myself with Google Calendar and email templates."**
> **Response:** "You're right - you could! But let me ask: how often do you forget to update that calendar? Or have to manually write a client email? 
>
> [Product Name] automates all of that. No more manual entry, no more forgetting deadlines, no more writer's block. It's the difference between driving a car and walking - both get you there, but one is a lot faster and easier. 🚗
>
> Give it a try - if you don't save at least 3 hours in the first week, I'll personally help you set up a manual system for free."

---

### Competitive Objections

**"I'm locked into a contract with [Competitor]."**
> **Response:** "Ugh, contracts are the worst. Here's what I'd suggest: Start a free trial with us in parallel. Use both tools for 2 weeks, then decide which one you actually prefer. 
>
> Most customers who switch tell us they wish they'd done it sooner. No pressure - just give it a shot and see if it's worth the switch when your contract ends."

**"My brokerage provides [Tool] for free."**
> **Response:** "That's awesome that your brokerage invests in tools! Quick question: do you actually use it? 
>
> A lot of agents tell us they have access to tools but don't use them because they're too complex or time-consuming. Our users love us because we're 10x simpler. Maybe use both for a week and see which one you actually open every day?"

---

### Timing Objections

**"I'm too busy right now."**
> **Response:** "I totally get it - busy season is crazy. But that's exactly when you need help the most, right? 
>
> Here's the thing: [Product Name] takes 5 minutes to set up, and then it reduces your workload. It's like hiring an assistant mid-peak-season. The longer you wait, the more hours you're spending on admin that could be automated.
>

**"I'll try it when I have more time."**
> **Response:** "Fair - but here's the trap: you'll never have 'more time.' 😅 The agents who benefit most are the ones who start during busy season because they see immediate relief.
>
> Can I offer you an extended 30-day trial instead of 14 days? That way there's no rush - try it when it fits your schedule, but you're locked in at our founding member rate."

## 3. Feature Deep Dives

### AI Contract Extraction

**How it works:**
- Uses GPT-4 Vision to "read" PDF contracts
- Extracts: closing date, contingency dates, parties, property info, price
- Confidence scoring (shows certainty level)
- Human review step for verification

**Common questions:**

Q: "How accurate is it?"  
A: "98%+ accuracy on standard contracts. If it's uncertain, it flags for your review. You always have final approval."

Q: "What if my contract is non-standard?"  
A: "The AI handles most variations well. If it struggles, you can manually enter or edit. We're constantly improving based on edge cases."

Q: "Is my contract data secure?"  
A: "Absolutely. Encrypted in transit and at rest. We never share or train on your data. Deleted on request."

---

### Smart Deadline Reminders

**How it works:**
- Automatic reminders at 7 days, 3 days, 1 day, and day-of
- Escalating urgency in messaging
- Daily digest email (summary of all deadlines)
- SMS notifications (Team Plan only)

**Common questions:**

Q: "Can I customize reminder timing?"  
A: "Yes! Go to Settings → Notifications to adjust. Some agents prefer 10/5/2 days, others want same-day only. It's flexible."

Q: "What if I don't want daily emails?"  
A: "You can turn off daily digest in Settings. Keep the deadline reminders, skip the daily summary. Your choice."

Q: "Do SMS reminders cost extra?"  
A: "They're included in Team Plan ($199/month). Professional and Starter plans are email-only."

---

### AI-Drafted Client Emails

**How it works:**
- Select transaction stage (e.g., "Inspection Complete")
- AI generates professional email based on transaction details
- Review, edit, and send
- Track opens and engagement

**Common questions:**

Q: "Can I customize the email templates?"  
A: "Absolutely. Go to Settings → Email Templates to adjust tone, add your branding, or write custom templates. The AI learns your preferences over time."

Q: "What if the AI writes something inaccurate?"  
A: "You always review before sending. If something's wrong, edit it or regenerate. The AI improves with feedback."

Q: "Can I schedule emails for later?"  
A: "Coming soon! For now, compose and send immediately. Scheduled sending is on our roadmap."

## 4. Troubleshooting Guide

### Issue: AI Extraction Failed

**Symptoms:** Error message after upload, or extraction shows "Low Confidence" for all fields

**Solutions:**
1. **Check file format:** Must be PDF (not images, Word docs, etc.)
2. **Check file size:** Under 10MB recommended
3. **Check contract quality:** Scanned contracts with handwriting may struggle
4. **Retry:** Sometimes API timeout - try again
5. **Manual entry:** If persistent, user can manually enter data

**Script:** "Sorry about that! Let's try re-uploading. If it still fails, we can manually enter the details - it takes 2 minutes. Also, what type of contract is this? I want to log this for our engineering team."

---

### Issue: Reminders Not Sending

**Symptoms:** User reports no email reminders despite upcoming deadlines

**Solutions:**
1. **Check spam folder:** Common issue - have them whitelist sender
2. **Check notification settings:** Settings → Notifications - are they enabled?
3. **Check email address:** Correct email on account?
4. **Check deadlines:** Are there actually upcoming deadlines?
5. **Resend test:** Use "Send test notification" in settings

**Script:** "Let's troubleshoot together. First, can you check your spam folder? If it's there, mark as 'Not Spam' and add us to your contacts. If not, let's check your notification settings..."

---

### Issue: Billing Problems

**Symptoms:** Payment failed, can't upgrade, wrong charge

**Solutions:**
1. **Check card:** Expired card? Wrong billing address?
2. **Retry:** Sometimes temporary bank block
3. **Contact Stripe:** If persistent, may need to contact payment processor
4. **Manual override:** For urgent cases, founder can manually adjust

**Script:** "I see the issue - [explanation]. Here's how to fix it: [steps]. If it persists, I can loop in our billing specialist to help. You won't lose access in the meantime."

## 5. Customer Health Management

### Health Score Components

Track these signals weekly:

1. **Engagement (40%)**
   - Logins per week (Target: 3+)
   - Transactions created (Target: 2+/month)
   - Contracts uploaded (Target: 2+/month)
   - Client emails sent (Target: 1+/month)

2. **Feature Adoption (30%)**
   - AI extraction used
   - Timeline viewed
   - Client emails sent
   - Document hub used
   - Settings customized

3. **Account Metrics (20%)**
   - Days since last login
   - Support tickets opened (more = unhealthy)
   - NPS score (if surveyed)
   - Payment failures

4. **Qualitative Signals (10%)**
   - Positive feedback
   - Feature requests (engaged users request features)
   - Referrals sent
   - Negative feedback / complaints

---

### Health Score Tiers

**🟢 Healthy (80-100)**
- Active daily/weekly usage
- Feature adoption > 70%
- No support issues
- Engaged and happy

**Action:** Ask for referrals, testimonials, case studies

---

**🟡 At Risk (50-79)**
- Irregular usage
- Feature adoption < 50%
- Some support tickets
- May be dissatisfied

**Action:** Proactive outreach, troubleshoot, offer onboarding call

---

**🔴 Critical (< 50)**
- Inactive > 14 days
- Feature adoption < 20%
- Multiple support issues
- Churn likely

**Action:** Immediate outreach, offer incentives, schedule intervention call

## 6. Escalation Procedures

### When to Escalate

Escalate to founder/engineering when:

1. **Technical bugs:** Core functionality broken (upload fails, extraction errors)
2. **Data loss:** User data missing or corrupted
3. **Security concerns:** Potential breach, unauthorized access
4. **Legal/compliance:** User threatens legal action, regulatory issues
5. **Major complaints:** User threatens to post negative reviews, file disputes
6. **Feature requests:** High-value customer requests critical feature

---

### Escalation Path

**Level 1: Customer Success / Support**
- First line of defense
- Handles 90% of issues
- Troubleshooting, FAQs, minor bugs

**Level 2: Product Manager / Founder**
- Complex issues
- Strategic customers
- Feature prioritization
- Billing disputes

**Level 3: Engineering**
- Critical bugs
- Data issues
- Security incidents
- Performance problems

---

### Escalation Script

**For User:**
"I want to make sure this gets the attention it deserves. I'm escalating this to our [Founder/Engineering team] right now. You can expect a response within [24 hours / 4 hours / immediately]. In the meantime, here's what I can do to help..."

**For Internal Team:**
"**[URGENT] Customer Escalation**

- Customer: [Name, email, plan tier]
- Issue: [Brief description]
- Impact: [High/Medium/Low]
- Customer sentiment: [Angry, Frustrated, Neutral]
- What I've tried: [Actions taken]
- Requested resolution: [What customer wants]

Slack: @[appropriate-person]
Ticket: [Link to support ticket]"
