---

## Behavioral Triggers

### If user hasn't uploaded first contract (Email 2):
- Send reminder 24 hours later
- Include 60-second video tutorial
- Offer personal onboarding call

### If user creates 5+ transactions in first week:
- Skip to Email 5 (Power User Tips)
- Consider upgrade email earlier

### If user is inactive for 5+ days:
- Send "We miss you" re-engagement email
- Offer help/troubleshooting

---

## A/B Test Ideas

1. **Subject lines:** "Welcome to [Product Name]" vs "Your AI transaction coordinator is ready"
2. **CTA buttons:** "Get Started" vs "Upload Your First Contract"
3. **Send times:** Morning vs afternoon
4. **Email length:** Short & punchy vs detailed guides
5. **Personalization:** With vs without usage data

---

## Success Metrics

Track these weekly:

- **Open rate:** Target > 45%
- **Click rate:** Target > 15%
- **Unsubscribe rate:** Target < 0.5%
- **Reply rate:** Target > 3% (engagement signal)
- **Time to first action:** How long after email do they complete the CTA?

---

## Notes

- Coordinate with Marketing on promotional emails to avoid conflict
- Pause sequence if user is in active support conversation
- Exclude users who churn during sequence
- Test different cadences (e.g., 10 days vs 14 days)
