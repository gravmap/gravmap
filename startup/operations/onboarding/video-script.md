# Getting Started Video Script

**Length:** 3 minutes  
**Format:** Screencast + voiceover  
**Purpose:** Show new users how to get value in under 5 minutes

---

## Video Structure

### 0:00-0:15 - Hook (15 seconds)
**Visual:** Product logo + quick montage of features  
**Audio:** "Stop spending hours on transaction admin. Let me show you how to go from contract to close in minutes, not hours."

---

### 0:15-0:45 - The Problem (30 seconds)
**Visual:** 
- Split screen: frustrated agent with piles of paper vs calm agent using laptop
- Show sticky notes, missed deadlines, scattered documents

**Audio:** "If you're a real estate agent, you know the pain. You close a deal, then spend the next 3 weeks managing deadlines, writing client emails, and hunting for documents across 5 different tools. Miss one deadline, and you could lose the deal - or your license."

---

### 0:45-1:30 - The Solution + Demo (45 seconds)
**Visual:** 
- Product dashboard
- Upload contract animation
- AI extraction progress bar
- Timeline appears

**Audio:** "Here's how [Product Name] changes everything. Watch this."

**[DEMO - Upload Contract]**

"I'm going to upload this purchase agreement. That's it - just drag and drop."

**[Show progress bar]**

"Our AI reads the contract and extracts all the key information: closing date, contingencies, parties, price - everything."

**[Show extraction review screen]**

"Here's what it found. I can confirm or edit in seconds. 98% of the time, it's perfect."

**[Show auto-generated timeline]**

"Boom - instant timeline. Every deadline, color-coded by urgency. No manual entry, no setup, no configuration."

---

### 1:30-2:15 - Deadline Reminders (45 seconds)
**Visual:**
- Timeline with upcoming deadlines
- Email notification example
- SMS notification example

**Audio:** "But here's where it gets really powerful. We don't just show you deadlines - we protect you from missing them."

**[Show email notification]**

"You'll get smart reminders: 7 days out, 3 days, 1 day, and the morning of. The messages get more urgent as the deadline approaches."

**[Show daily digest]**

"Every morning, get a summary of all upcoming deadlines across all your transactions. No more surprises."

**[Show calendar integration if Team Plan]**

"And if you're on the Team Plan, add everything to Google Calendar with one click."

---

### 2:15-2:45 - Client Updates (30 seconds)
**Visual:**
- Client communication feature
- AI drafting email
- One-click send

**Audio:** "Now let's talk about the 15-20 minutes you spend writing client status updates. Gone."

**[Show AI drafting email]**

"Click 'Send Client Update,' choose the stage - like 'Inspection Complete' - and our AI writes a professional, specific email for you. Review it, customize if you want, and send."

**[Show sent email]**

"You just saved 20 minutes. Multiply that by 5 transactions a month, and you've saved almost 2 hours a week just on emails."

---

### 2:45-3:00 - Call to Action (15 seconds)
**Visual:** 
- User at dashboard with 3 transactions
- Smiling agent
- CTA button

**Audio:** "That's it. Upload a contract, review the timeline, send updates. Three minutes of work for what used to take you an hour."

**[Text on screen]**

"You're ready. Go upload your first contract and see the magic happen."

**[Button]**

"Get started now - [website URL]"

## Production Notes

### Tone
- **Friendly but efficient:** No fluff, get straight to value
- **Confident:** Show, don't just tell
- **Relatable:** Use real agent pain points

### Visual Style
- **Clean screencast:** Zoom in on important features
- **Annotations:** Use arrows and highlights to draw attention
- **Pacing:** Fast enough to not bore, slow enough to follow

### Voiceover
- **Record yourself first:** Founder voice is authentic
- **Hire pro later:** Once script is validated
- **Background music:** Light, upbeat, non-distracting

### Tools
- **Loom:** Quick recordings, easy sharing
- **ScreenFlow / Camtasia:** Professional editing
- **Descript:** AI-powered editing, easy transcripts

## Variations

### 60-Second Version (For Ads)
- Cut the problem section
- Go straight to demo
- Focus on ONE feature (e.g., just AI extraction or just client updates)

### 10-Minute Deep Dive (For Help Center)
- Expand each section
- Show more features (document hub, search, settings)
- Include troubleshooting tips

### 30-Second Teaser (For Social Media)
- Quick montage of features
- No voiceover, just text on screen + music
- End with "Try free for 14 days"

## Analytics Tracking

Track these metrics:

1. **Play rate:** What % of visitors click play?
2. **Average watch time:** Where do they drop off?
3. **Completion rate:** What % watch to the end?
4. **Click-through rate:** What % visit signup after watching?
5. **Conversion rate:** What % sign up after watching?

**Optimization:**
- If drop-off at 0:30, shorten intro
- If drop-off at 2:00, make demo faster
- If low CTR, improve CTA clarity

---

## Script Variations (A/B Testing)

### Variation A: Problem-First (This script)
- Start with pain points
- Build empathy
- Then show solution

### Variation B: Solution-First
- Start with demo immediately
- "Watch how easy this is"
- Explain benefits as you go

### Variation C: Story-First
- "Meet Sarah, a real estate agent in Austin..."
- Tell a story of transformation
- Product is the hero

**Recommendation:** Start with Problem-First (Variation A), test others later.

## Accessibility

- **Captions:** Auto-generate, then manually review
- **Transcript:** Provide full text below video
- **Audio description:** Consider for visually impaired users
- **Playback speed:** Allow 1.25x, 1.5x, 2x options
