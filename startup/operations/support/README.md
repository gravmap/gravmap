# Support Documentation

**Purpose:** Provide world-class customer support through clear documentation, efficient escalation procedures, and standardized response templates

**Goal:** Resolve 80% of issues in first contact, < 24-hour response time, > 90% customer satisfaction

---

## Components

1. **Help Center Articles** - Expanded beyond MVP basics
2. **Support Escalation Procedures** - Clear path for complex issues
3. **Response Templates** - Pre-written responses for common issues
4. **SLA Definitions** - Service level agreements by plan tier

---

## Support Philosophy

### The "First Contact Resolution" Goal
- Aim to resolve 80%+ of issues in the first response
- Empower customers with self-service when possible
- Anticipate follow-up questions and answer them upfront

### Support Tiers

**Tier 0: Self-Service**
- Help center articles
- In-app tooltips
- Video tutorials
- Community forum (future)

**Tier 1: Customer Success Team**
- Email support (all plans)
- In-app chat (all plans)
- Basic troubleshooting
- Feature questions

**Tier 2: Product/Engineering**
- Technical issues
- Bug reports
- Complex integrations
- Data problems

**Tier 3: Founder/Executive**
- Enterprise customers
- Critical incidents
- Legal/compliance issues
- Major escalations

---

## Response Time Targets

| Plan | Initial Response | Follow-Up | Critical Issues |
|------|------------------|-----------|-----------------|
| Starter | 24 hours | 48 hours | 8 hours |
| Professional | 12 hours | 24 hours | 4 hours |
| Team | 4 hours | 12 hours | 2 hours |
| Enterprise (future) | 1 hour | 4 hours | 1 hour |

---

## Support Channels

1. **In-App Chat (Crisp/Intercom)** - Best for quick questions
2. **Email (support@[domain].com)** - Best for complex issues requiring screenshots
3. **Phone** - Team Plan only, for urgent issues
4. **Help Center** - Self-service 24/7
