### Article 2.1: Uploading Contracts

**Title:** How to Upload a Contract

**Content:**

Uploading a contract is the first step to automating your transaction management.

#### Step-by-Step:

1. **Go to Dashboard** → Click "New Transaction"

2. **Enter Basic Info** (optional):
   - Transaction nickname (e.g., "123 Main St - Smith")
   - This helps you identify it later

3. **Upload Contract**:
   - Drag & drop PDF onto upload area, OR
   - Click "Browse Files" to select from your computer

4. **AI Extraction Begins**:
   - You'll see a progress bar
   - Takes 10-30 seconds depending on contract length

   - AI shows extracted details with confidence scores
   - Green = high confidence, Yellow = medium, Red = low
   - Edit any fields if needed
   - Click "Confirm" to proceed

6. **Timeline Generated**:
   - Your transaction timeline appears automatically
   - All deadlines are set based on contract dates

#### Supported Formats:
- ✅ PDF (best results)
- ❌ Word docs, images, scans (may reduce accuracy)

#### File Size Limit:
- Maximum: 10MB per file
- Typical contracts: 1-3MB

**Troubleshooting:**
- **Upload fails?** Check file size and format
- **Extraction error?** Ensure contract is legible, not handwritten
- **Wrong data?** You can always edit after confirmation

### Article 2.5: Archiving vs Deleting Transactions

**Title:** Should I Archive or Delete a Transaction?

**Content:**

#### Archive (Recommended)

**What it does:**
- Moves transaction out of "Active" view
- Keeps all data, documents, and history
- Can be unarchived later if needed

**When to use:**
- Transaction closed successfully
- Transaction fell through but you want records
- Transaction on hold indefinitely

**How to archive:**
1. Open transaction
2. Click "..." menu → "Archive Transaction"
3. Confirm

**Where do archived transactions go?**
- Dashboard → Filter → "Archived"
- Accessible but not in your way

#### Delete (Permanent)

**What it does:**
- Permanently removes transaction and all data
- Cannot be recovered
- Documents, emails, timeline all deleted

**When to use:**
- Test transactions you created for practice
- Duplicate transactions
- Wrong property uploaded by mistake

**How to delete:**
1. Open transaction
2. Click "..." menu → "Delete Transaction"
3. Type "DELETE" to confirm (prevents accidents)

**Warning:** Only delete if you're absolutely sure. Archive is safer.

#### Best Practice:
- Archive closed transactions at end of each month
- Keep active + archived for 7 years (industry standard)
- Delete only test/duplicate transactions

### Article 4.4: Best Practices for Client Communication

**Title:** Client Communication Tips from Top Agents

**Content:**

Our most successful users follow these best practices:

#### 1. Send Updates Proactively

**Don't wait for clients to ask.** Send updates at every major milestone:
- ✅ Contract accepted
- ✅ Inspection scheduled/complete
- ✅ Appraisal complete
- ✅ Clear to close
- ✅ Closing complete

**Why:** Clients feel informed and confident. Reduces anxiety calls.

#### 2. Be Specific

**Generic:** "Everything is moving along."  
**Specific:** "The inspection is scheduled for Tuesday at 2 PM. I'll send you the report within 24 hours."

**Why:** Specificity builds trust and reduces questions.

#### 3. Set Expectations

**Include:**
- What just happened
- What happens next
- When they'll hear from you again

**Example:** "The inspection is done. Next, the appraiser will visit the property within 5-7 days. I'll update you as soon as we have the appraisal report."

#### 4. Respond Quickly

**If clients reply to your update:**
- Respond within 2-4 hours (during business hours)
- Even if just to say "Got it, I'll find out and get back to you"

**Why:** Fast responses reduce anxiety.

#### 5. Over-Communicate

**It's better to send too many updates than too few.** Clients want to know you're on top of things.

**Rule of thumb:** If something happens, send an update. Even if it seems minor.

#### 6. Use the Templates, Then Customize

**AI templates are a starting point.** Add:
- Personal details
- Your personality
- Specific context for that client

**The best emails:** 80% AI + 20% personalization
