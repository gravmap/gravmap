## Customization Tips

### Make Templates Your Own:

1. **Add personalization:**
   - "[Name]" not "Dear Customer"
   - Reference their specific situation
   - Use their plan tier

2. **Adjust tone:**
   - Frustrated customer? More empathetic
   - Happy customer? More enthusiastic
   - Technical user? More detailed

3. **Add context:**
   - "I see you're on the Professional Plan"
   - "I noticed you've uploaded 12 transactions"
   - "You mentioned you're in California"

4. **End with next steps:**
   - "I'll follow up in 24 hours"
   - "Please reply with..."
   - "Here's what I'm doing..."
