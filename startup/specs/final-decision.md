## EXACT MVP SCOPE

### What We BUILD (2 Weeks):

**Core Value Proposition:**
"Upload your contracts. AI extracts every deadline. You never miss a date again."

**Feature 1: Document Upload & Storage**
- Drag-and-drop PDF upload
- Organize by transaction (property address)
- Basic folder structure (contracts, disclosures, inspections, etc.)
- Secure storage (encrypted at rest)

**Feature 2: AI Document Intelligence** ⭐ CORE DIFFERENTIATOR
- Extract key dates automatically:
  - Contract acceptance date
  - Inspection contingency deadline
  - Financing contingency deadline
  - Appraisal deadline
  - Closing date
- Extract parties: Buyer name, seller name, agent info
- Extract property details: Address, price, MLS number
- Confidence scoring (user reviews before saving)

**Feature 3: Intelligent Timeline Generator**
- Auto-generate transaction timeline from extracted dates
- Visual calendar view
- Color-coded deadlines (red = urgent, yellow = upcoming, green = completed)
- Business days calculation (no weekends/holidays for deadlines)

**Feature 4: Deadline Reminders**
- Email reminders at 7 days, 3 days, 1 day before each deadline
- Daily digest email: "Here's what's due this week"
- Mobile-responsive (check on phone)

**Feature 5: Client Communication Templates**
- Pre-written email templates for common updates:
  - "Contract accepted"
  - "Inspection complete"
  - "Clear to close"
  - "Closing tomorrow"
- Auto-fill with transaction details
- One-click copy to clipboard

**Feature 6: Basic Dashboard**
- Active transactions list
- Overdue items (red alert)
- Upcoming this week
- Quick search by address/client

## RISK MITIGATION PLAN

### Risk 1: High Competition
**Mitigation:**
- Focus on niche incumbents ignore (solo agents)
- AI-first architecture = 2-3 year moat
- Move fast (ship in 2 weeks, iterate weekly)
- Build community and trust (incumbents are faceless corporations)

### Risk 2: High Churn
**Mitigation:**
- Onboard every customer personally (first 100)
- Show ROI immediately (save 1+ hours in first week)
- Build habit loops (daily email digest)
- Pricing at $49/month = low enough to stick around
- Target agents with 15+ deals/year (consistent usage)

### Risk 3: Distribution Difficulty
**Mitigation:**
- Start with warm network (LinkedIn connections)
- Community-first approach (Reddit, Facebook groups)
- Content marketing (blog, YouTube, TikTok)
- Partner with real estate coaches (revenue share)
- Free lead magnet (transaction checklist generator)
- Avoid paid ads until product-market fit proven

### Risk 4: AI Accuracy
**Mitigation:**
- Human-in-the-loop (user reviews all extractions)
- Start with ONE state (California - standard forms)
- Confidence scoring (flag low-confidence extractions)
- Quick feedback loop (user corrects → model improves)
- Clear expectations: "AI assists, you verify"

### Risk 5: Feature Creep
**Mitigation:**
- Rigid scope for MVP (deadline management ONLY)
- Say NO to 90% of feature requests initially
- User voting system for roadmap (but CEO decides)
- Focus metric: % of users who say "I'd be disappointed if this disappeared"

### Risk 6: Revenue Ceiling
**Mitigation:**
- Accept micro-SaaS reality ($5-10M ARR is SUCCESS)
- Not building venture-scale startup
- Lifestyle business is fine
- Multiple expansion paths if we hit ceiling:
  - Add team features (move upmarket)
  - Add coaching/consulting (services revenue)
  - Expand to other verticals (mortgage, title, insurance)
  - Sell to incumbent (acqui-hire)
