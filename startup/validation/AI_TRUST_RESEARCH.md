## AI Adoption: HIGH ✅

- **82% of agents** use AI for listing descriptions (up from 58%)
- **74% use AI** for marketing content
- **75%+ of firms** planning to increase AI investments
- **Market size:** $488M for gen AI in real estate by 2025

**Key insight:** AI adoption is mainstream. Not early adopters anymore.

## Trust Concerns: MODERATE ⚠️

### Major Concerns:

1. **Accuracy**
   - AI valuation models undervalue properties
   - Lacks local knowledge
   - Misses renovations/features
   - Agents double-check AI outputs

2. **Human Element**
   - "AI cannot replicate human empathy"
   - Buying/selling is emotional
   - Trust requires human connection
   - Complex scenarios need human judgment

3. **Data Privacy**
   - Concerns about how AI processes information
   - Confidentiality issues
   - Need transparency with clients
   - Compliance/liability questions

4. **Governance**
   - RETTC released AI Governance Framework (2025)
   - Principles: fairness, transparency, data integrity
   - Agents must be careful using AI for clients

### Positioning:
- "AI that handles the 'how', you handle the 'why'"
- Focus on accuracy guarantees
- Emphasize human oversight/review
- Clear privacy policy
- Transparent about AI use

## Key Quotes:

"Agents must exercise extreme care when using AI on behalf of clients, including double-checking outputs for accuracy and confidentiality"
