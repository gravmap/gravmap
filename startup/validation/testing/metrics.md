# Success Metrics & Thresholds

**What Numbers Mean "Validated" vs "Pivot"?**

---

## The Validation Framework

We measure success at 4 stages:

1. **Problem Validation** (Before building)
2. **Solution Validation** (With demo/prototype)
3. **Early Traction** (First 50 customers)
4. **Product-Market Fit** (Month 3+)

Each stage has specific thresholds. Miss the threshold = pivot or kill.

---

## Stage 1: Problem Validation (Pre-Build)

**Goal:** Confirm the problem exists and is painful enough to pay for.

### Metrics & Thresholds

| Metric | Validated ✅ | Concern ⚠️ | Invalidated ❌ | Pivot Signal |
|--------|-------------|-----------|---------------|--------------|
| **Conversations Completed** | 10+ | 5-9 | <5 | Can't find people to talk to |
| **Confirm Problem Exists** | 7+ (70%) | 4-6 (40-69%) | <4 (<40%) | They don't have this problem |
| **Rank Problem Top-3** | 5+ (50%) | 3-4 (30-49%) | <3 (<30%) | Wrong problem to solve |
| **Tried Solutions Before** | 5+ (50%) | 2-4 (20-49%) | <2 (<20%) | Not painful enough |
| **Willing to Pay $50+/mo** | 5+ (50%) | 2-4 (20-49%) | <2 (<20%) | Not worth paying for |

### Decision Framework

**GO Signal (All must be true):**
- ✅ 10+ conversations completed
- ✅ 70%+ confirm problem exists
- ✅ 50%+ rank it top-3 pain point
- ✅ 50%+ have tried to solve it
- ✅ 50%+ willing to pay

**PIVOT Signal (Any of these):**
- ❌ Can't find 10 people to talk to (wrong market?)
- ❌ <40% confirm problem exists (wrong problem?)
- ❌ <30% rank it top-3 (not painful enough)
- ❌ <20% tried solutions (not urgent)
- ❌ <20% willing to pay (no budget)

**What to Pivot To:**
- Different problem in real estate
- Different segment (solo agents vs teams)
- Different industry with same problem

## Stage 2: Solution Validation (Demo/Prototype)

**Goal:** Confirm users want YOUR solution, not just any solution.

### Metrics & Thresholds

| Metric | Validated ✅ | Concern ⚠️ | Invalidated ❌ | Pivot Signal |
|--------|-------------|-----------|---------------|--------------|
| **Demo Completion** | 8+ (80%) | 5-7 (50-79%) | <5 (<50%) | Demo is confusing |
| **Upload Real Contract** | 6+ (60%) | 3-5 (30-59%) | <3 (<30%) | Don't trust product |
| **Say "I'd Use This"** | 7+ (70%) | 4-6 (40-69%) | <4 (<40%) | Don't see value |
| **Ask "When Can I Start?"** | 4+ (40%) | 2-3 (20-39%) | <2 (<20%) | Not excited |
| **Offer to Pay** | 2+ (20%) | 1 (10-19%) | 0 (<10%) | Not valuable enough |
| **Extraction Accuracy** | >90% | 70-90% | <70% | AI doesn't work |

### Decision Framework

**GO Signal (All must be true):**
- ✅ 80%+ complete the demo
- ✅ 60%+ upload a real contract
- ✅ 70%+ say they'd use it
- ✅ 40%+ ask when they can start
- ✅ 20%+ offer to pay immediately
- ✅ AI accuracy >90%

**PIVOT Signal (Any of these):**
- ❌ <50% complete demo (too confusing)
- ❌ <30% upload real contract (trust issue)
- ❌ <40% say they'd use it (wrong solution)
- ❌ <20% ask to start (not excited)
- ❌ 0 offer to pay (not valuable)
- ❌ AI accuracy <70% (technical failure)

**What to Pivot To:**
- Simplify onboarding (if completion low)
- Add trust signals (if upload low)
- Different features (if value unclear)
- Non-AI solution (if accuracy fails)

## Stage 3: Early Traction (First 50 Customers)

**Goal:** Confirm people will actually use and pay for the product.

### Metrics & Thresholds

| Metric | Validated ✅ | Concern ⚠️ | Invalidated ❌ | Pivot Signal |
|--------|-------------|-----------|---------------|--------------|
| **Signups (Week 1)** | 20+ | 10-19 | <10 | No interest |
| **Activation Rate** | >60% | 40-59% | <40% | Signup ≠ activation |
| **Upload Real Contract** | >50% | 30-49% | <30% | Not trusting/using |
| **Week 1 Retention** | >70% | 50-69% | <50% | Tried once, quit |
| **Paying Customers** | 10+ | 5-9 | <5 | Won't pay |
| **Time to First Value** | <5 min | 5-10 min | >10 min | Too slow |
| **Customer Acq Cost** | <$100 | $100-200 | >$200 | Too expensive |
| **Monthly Churn** | <15% | 15-25% | >25% | Don't stay |

### Decision Framework

**GO Signal (All must be true):**
- ✅ 20+ signups in first week
- ✅ 60%+ activation rate
- ✅ 50%+ upload real contract
- ✅ 70%+ return after first week
- ✅ 10+ paying customers
- ✅ <5 min to first value
- ✅ <$100 CAC
- ✅ <15% monthly churn

**PIVOT Signal (Any of these):**
- ❌ <10 signups (no interest)
- ❌ <40% activation (signup ≠ usage)
- ❌ <30% upload real data (not using)
- ❌ <50% week 1 retention (not sticky)
- ❌ <5 paying customers (won't pay)
- ❌ >10 min to value (too slow)
- ❌ >$200 CAC (unsustainable)
- ❌ >25% monthly churn (don't stay)

**What to Pivot To:**
- Different marketing channel (if CAC high)
- Simplify onboarding (if activation low)
- Fix core value prop (if retention low)
- Different pricing (if conversion low)

## Stage 4: Product-Market Fit (Month 3+)

**Goal:** Confirm you have a sustainable, scalable business.

### The PMF Test (Sean Ellis)

**"How would you feel if you could no longer use [Product]?"**

| Response | PMF Signal |
|----------|-----------|
| Very disappointed | ✅ Strong PMF |
| Somewhat disappointed | ⚠️ Warning |
| Not disappointed | ❌ No PMF |

**Threshold:** 40%+ "very disappointed" = PMF

### Metrics & Thresholds

| Metric | PMF ✅ | Approaching ⚠️ | No PMF ❌ | Pivot Signal |
|--------|-------|---------------|-----------|--------------|
| **"Very Disappointed"** | >40% | 25-39% | <25% | Not a must-have |
| **Monthly Churn** | <10% | 10-15% | >15% | Not sticky |
| **Weekly Active Users** | >60% | 40-59% | <40% | Not engaging |
| **Organic Signups** | >30% | 15-29% | <15% | No word-of-mouth |
| **NPS Score** | >50 | 30-49 | <30 | Not happy |
| **Revenue Churn** | <7% | 7-12% | >12% | Downgrading |
| **LTV:CAC Ratio** | >3:1 | 1:1-3:1 | <1:1 | Unit economics fail |
| **MRR Growth** | >15%/mo | 5-15%/mo | <5%/mo | Stalling |

### Decision Framework

**PMF ACHIEVED (All must be true):**
- ✅ 40%+ "very disappointed"
- ✅ <10% monthly churn
- ✅ >60% weekly active users
- ✅ >30% organic signups
- ✅ NPS >50
- ✅ LTV:CAC >3:1
- ✅ MRR growing 15%+/month

**APPROACHING PMF (Most true, some concerns):**
- ⚠️ 25-39% "very disappointed"
- ⚠️ 10-15% churn
- ⚠️ 40-59% weekly active
- ⚠️ 15-29% organic
- ⚠️ NPS 30-49
- ⚠️ LTV:CAC 1:1-3:1

**NO PMF (Multiple failures):**
- ❌ <25% "very disappointed"
- ❌ >15% churn
- ❌ <40% weekly active
- ❌ <15% organic signups
- ❌ NPS <30
- ❌ LTV:CAC <1:1

**What to Do:**
- **PMF Achieved:** Scale! Invest in growth.
- **Approaching PMF:** Iterate, don't scale. Fix the gaps.
- **No PMF:** Pivot or kill. Don't scale.

## Pricing Validation

### Willingness to Pay Thresholds

| Price Point | % Who Say "Yes" | Signal |
|-------------|-----------------|--------|
| **$49/month** | >70% | ✅ Easy sale |
| **$99/month** | >50% | ✅ Reasonable |
| **$149/month** | >30% | ⚠️ May need more value |
| **$199/month** | >20% | ⚠️ Premium segment only |
| **$299+/month** | >10% | ❌ Too high for most |

### Actual Payment vs. Stated Willingness

**The Truth Test:**
- 100 people say "I'd pay $99/month"
- 20 people actually pay when asked
- **Real WTP: 20% at $99**

**Always validate with actual payments, not surveys.**

## Retention Cohort Analysis

### Target Retention Curves

| Cohort | Week 1 | Week 2 | Week 4 | Week 8 | Week 12 |
|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|---------|
| **Good** | 80% | 70% | 60% | 50% | 45% |

**If retention flattens (week 8-12 stable), you have PMF.**
**If retention keeps dropping, no PMF.**

## Acquisition Channel Validation

### Channel Viability Thresholds

| Channel | CAC Target | Payback Target | Signal |
|---------|-----------|----------------|--------|
| **Organic/Referral** | $0-25 | <1 month | ✅ Best channel |
| **Content/SEO** | $25-75 | 1-2 months | ✅ Great |
| **Direct Sales** | $50-150 | 2-3 months | ✅ Good (if scalable) |
| **Paid Ads** | $75-150 | 2-4 months | ⚠️ Needs optimization |
| **Partnerships** | $25-100 | 1-3 months | ✅ Good if repeatable |
| **Events/Conferences** | $100-300 | 3-6 months | ⚠️ Expensive |

**Rule:** CAC must be < 1/3 of LTV for channel to be viable.

## Kill Criteria Summary

### Instant Kill (Stop Immediately)

1. **Can't find 10 people to talk to** → Wrong market
2. **<40% confirm problem exists** → Wrong problem
3. **<20% willing to pay anything** → No business
4. **AI extraction <70% accurate after iteration** → Technical failure
5. **0 paying customers after 30 days** → No demand

### Pivot After Iteration (Try 2-3 times)

6. **<40% say they'd use it** → Wrong solution
7. **>25% monthly churn** → Not sticky
8. **<25% "very disappointed"** → Nice-to-have, not must-have
9. **CAC > LTV** → Unit economics broken
10. **No organic growth after 50 customers** → No word-of-mouth

### Persevere Signals (Keep Going)

- 40%+ "very disappointed"
- <10% monthly churn
- Organic referrals happening
- LTV:CAC >3:1
- MRR growing 15%+/month
- Users asking for more features

## The Brutal Truth About Numbers

**Vanity numbers:**
- Total signups (without activation)
- Page views (without conversion)
- Social followers (without engagement)
- Features built (without usage)

**Real numbers:**
- Activated users (who actually use it)
- Paying customers (who give you money)
- Retained users (who keep coming back)
- Referring users (who tell others)

**Only care about:**
1. Can we get users? (Acquisition)
2. Will they pay? (Monetization)
3. Will they stay? (Retention)
4. Will they tell others? (Referral)

If the answer to all 4 is "yes," you have a business.

## Next Steps

1. **Track metrics from day 1** (set up analytics)
2. **Review weekly** (don't wait too long)
3. **Be honest about failures** (no rationalizing)
4. **Pivot fast if needed** (don't fall in love with solution)
5. **Celebrate learning** (even from failures)

**The goal: Find the truth as fast as possible.**

If PMF exists, scale. If not, pivot or kill. Simple.
