## KILL CRITERIA CHECKLIST

**Check weekly - if any are TRUE, consider pivot:**

### Problem Validation Kill Signals
- [ ] Can't find 10 people to talk to
- [ ] <40% confirm problem exists
- [ ] <20% willing to pay anything
- [ ] Problem not in top-5 pain points

### Solution Validation Kill Signals
- [ ] <50% complete demo
- [ ] <30% upload real contract
- [ ] <40% say they'd use it
- [ ] 0 people offer to pay
- [ ] AI accuracy <70%

### Early Traction Kill Signals
- [ ] <10 signups in week 1
- [ ] <40% activation rate
- [ ] <30% upload real data
- [ ] <50% week 1 retention
- [ ] <5 paying customers after 30 days
- [ ] >25% monthly churn

### PMF Kill Signals
- [ ] <25% "very disappointed"
- [ ] >15% monthly churn
- [ ] <40% weekly active users
- [ ] <15% organic signups
- [ ] LTV:CAC <1:1
- [ ] 0 organic referrals after 50 customers

**If ANY are checked:** STOP and reassess. Don't scale.

## REMEMBER

**This scorecard exists to prevent self-deception.**

- Don't rationalize bad numbers
- Don't skip tracking
- Don't ignore patterns
- Don't celebrate vanity metrics

**The goal is truth, not validation.**

If the numbers say "pivot," pivot.
If the numbers say "kill," kill.
If the numbers say "scale," scale.

**Trust the data, not your feelings.**
